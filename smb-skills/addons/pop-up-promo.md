# pop-up-promo.md

## Purpose

Display a promotional offer when a visitor is about to leave the website.

Use this addon when the business wants to increase conversions through special offers, discounts, coupons, or limited-time promotions.

This addon should be used sparingly.

Avoid interrupting visitors who are actively browsing the website.

---

## Trigger

Desktop:

Display the popup when exit intent is detected.

Examples:

* Cursor moves toward the browser close button
* Cursor moves outside the top edge of the browser window

Mobile:

Do not automatically display the popup unless specifically requested.

Exit intent detection is unreliable on mobile devices.

---

## Popup Content

Keep the popup simple.

Include:

* Headline
* Short supporting text
* Offer or promotion
* CTA button
* Close button

Examples:

* 10% Off First Service
* Free Estimate
* Free Inspection
* Limited-Time Promotion
* Seasonal Offer

Avoid long paragraphs.

The offer should be immediately understandable.

---

## Layout

Desktop:

Centered modal.

Dim the background.

Keep the popup compact.

Mobile:

Full-width card or modal.

Ensure the close button remains easily accessible.

---

## CTA

Examples:

* Claim Offer
* Get My Discount
* Schedule Service
* Request Quote
* Contact Us

The CTA should lead directly to the desired conversion action.

Examples:

* Contact form
* Booking form
* Phone call
* Quote request

---

## Display Rules

Display the popup only once per visitor session.

Do not repeatedly show the popup after dismissal.

Respect the user's choice to close the popup.

---

## Design Guidelines

Follow the design system defined in `smb-design.md`.

Use the website's branding and color palette.

Avoid aggressive animations and attention-grabbing effects.

The popup should feel professional and trustworthy.

---

## Completion

Confirm:

* Exit intent detection works correctly.
* The popup displays only once per session.
* The close button functions correctly.
* The CTA leads to the intended conversion action.
