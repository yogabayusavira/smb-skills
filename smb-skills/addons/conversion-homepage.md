# conversion-homepage.md

## Purpose

Convert the standard homepage into a conversion-first homepage.

Use this addon when lead generation is the primary goal.

This addon modifies the homepage Hero section only.

All other layouts and design rules remain defined by `smb-design.md`.

---

## Hero Layout Override

Replace the default Hero layout.

Desktop:

Left Side:

* Headline
* Supporting text
* Primary CTA
* Trust indicators if available

Right Side:

* Contact form

Mobile:

* Headline
* Supporting text
* Primary CTA
* Contact form

The contact form should remain visible without requiring navigation to another page.

---

## Hero Contact Form

Purpose:

Allow visitors to immediately request service, a quote, or a callback.

Form Fields:

* Name
* Phone
* Email
* Message

Keep the form short.

Do not add unnecessary fields.

Use a placeholder implementation.

Do not assume a specific provider or backend.

---

## Trust Indicators

Place trust indicators close to the form when possible.

Examples:

* Google Rating
* Review Count
* Years in Business
* Licensed & Insured
* Emergency Service Available

Trust should support the contact decision.

---

## CTA Priority

Prioritize contact actions throughout the homepage.

Examples:

* Get a Quote
* Request Service
* Schedule Service
* Book Appointment
* Contact Us

The homepage should encourage contact without becoming overly aggressive.

---

## Completion

Apply this addon on top of the existing homepage structure defined in `smb-design.md`.
