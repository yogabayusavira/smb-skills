# branding.md

## Purpose

Apply the business branding to the website.

The website structure, layout, and functionality should already be completed before this step.

Focus only on visual identity and branding assets.

---

## Collect Branding Assets

Ask the user to provide any available branding assets.

Examples:

* Logo
* Alternate logo variations
* Favicon
* Brand guidelines
* Business photos
* Team photos
* Service photos
* Location photos

If branding assets are unavailable, continue using the design system defined in `smb-design.md`.

---

## Logo

Use the provided logo throughout the website.

Common placements:

* Navigation
* Footer
* Favicon
* Social sharing image

Use the highest quality version available.

Prefer SVG files when available.

---

## Color Palette

Use the logo as the primary source for the website color palette.

Extract colors from the logo when possible.

Prioritize:

* Primary brand color
* Secondary brand color
* Accent color

Maintain strong contrast and readability.

Do not sacrifice usability for brand consistency.

If no logo is available, choose professional colors appropriate for the business industry.

---

## Typography

If the business has established typography guidelines, follow them.

Otherwise, use clean and highly readable fonts.

Prioritize readability over uniqueness.

---

## Images

Use real business photos whenever possible.

Prioritize:

* Team photos
* Owner photos
* Service photos
* Project photos
* Business location photos

If real photos are unavailable, use relevant Unsplash images as placeholders.

Avoid generic stock photography whenever possible.

---

## Favicon

Create and configure a favicon.

Use the business logo when appropriate.

Ensure favicon assets are included in the project.

---

## Social Sharing Image

Create a social sharing image for:

* Facebook
* LinkedIn
* X
* Messaging applications

The image should include:

* Business logo
* Business name
* Relevant visual branding

---

## Brand Consistency Review

Confirm that:

* Colors are consistent
* Typography is consistent
* Images are consistent
* Logo usage is consistent
* Branding matches the business type

The website should feel like a real business website rather than a generic template.

---

## Completion

Confirm the setup is complete.

After confirmation, continue to `smb-skills/finalize.md`.
